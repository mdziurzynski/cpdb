Welcome to the Curated Primers Database


CPDb gathers, verifies and compares properties of hundreds of primer pairs published over the years. At the moment, we present primer pairs dedicated to identification of antibiotic resistance genes. In the near future, the database will be extended to include heavy metals resistance and taxonomy marker gene primers.
